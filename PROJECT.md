# Barcelona Relocation Dashboard

## Mission
Build a private, local-first relocation planning dashboard for moving from Malden, Missouri to Barcelona, Spain with my wife and our three-year-old child around January 2027.

The system should help us organize many moving parts, compare options, track decisions, estimate budgets, visualize the roadmap, and create conversation prompts for planning discussions.

## Desired Outcome
A visually appealing localhost web app that my wife and I can use as our relocation command center.

The app should help us:
- See a clear roadmap from now through the move.
- Track decisions, options, ideas, and open questions.
- Maintain a budget and financial plan.
- Compare relocation options.
- Track documents, tasks, risks, and dependencies.
- Export snapshots and PDFs so we can preserve our current state over time.
- Use the dashboard as a conversation tool during family planning discussions.

## Users
- Primary updater: Nate.
- Collaborator/viewer: my wife.
- Family context: we have a three-year-old child, so childcare, schooling, healthcare, neighborhood safety, travel logistics, and family stability matter.

## Constraints
- Localhost-only. Do not publish online.
- Private family planning data. Avoid cloud dependencies unless explicitly approved.
- Easy to update manually.
- Visually appealing and usable by non-technical family members.
- Exportable snapshots are important.
- Must support multiple planning tracks at once.
- Should be flexible because our plans will change as we learn more.
- Target move timing: January 2027.
- Origin: Malden, Missouri.
- Destination: Barcelona, Spain.

## Planning Tracks
- Immigration and visa options.
- Budget and savings plan.
- Housing and neighborhoods.
- Healthcare and insurance.
- Childcare, preschool, and family support.
- Travel and logistics.
- Work, income, and business considerations.
- Documents and legal requirements.
- Language and cultural preparation.
- Pet/family belongings if applicable.
- Timeline and milestone roadmap.
- Risks, unknowns, and contingency plans.

## Product Vision
This is a local family relocation command center.

It should be part:
- visual roadmap
- budget planner
- decision journal
- option tracker
- document checklist
- idea board
- conversation guide
- exportable snapshot archive

## Initial Build Direction
Start with a local web app that can run on localhost.

Preferred early design:
- Dashboard homepage.
- Roadmap/timeline view.
- Budget view.
- Decisions view.
- Options comparison view.
- Tasks/checklist view.
- Export/snapshot view.

Data should initially be simple and portable, such as JSON files or SQLite. Avoid complex infrastructure until needed.
