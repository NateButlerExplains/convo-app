# Product Vision

## Working Name
Barcelona Relocation Command Center

## What This Is
A private local web app for planning a family move from Malden, Missouri to Barcelona, Spain.

It is designed to help Nate and his wife have better planning conversations by making the roadmap, budget, decisions, options, risks, and open questions visible in one place.

## Core Views

### 1. Roadmap View
A visual timeline from now through January 2027.

Possible phases:
- Research and feasibility.
- Financial planning.
- Visa/residency preparation.
- Housing and neighborhood exploration.
- Healthcare and childcare planning.
- Document preparation.
- Travel logistics.
- Final move preparation.
- Arrival and stabilization.

### 2. Budget View
Track estimated, planned, and actual costs.

Categories:
- Visa/legal.
- Flights.
- Housing deposits.
- Temporary lodging.
- Monthly living expenses.
- Healthcare/insurance.
- Childcare/schooling.
- Shipping/storage.
- Emergency fund.
- Local transportation.
- Language/cultural prep.
- Miscellaneous.

### 3. Decisions View
Track decisions with rationale.

Fields:
- Decision.
- Status: proposed, leaning, decided, revisiting.
- Options considered.
- Rationale.
- Date.
- Who needs to agree.
- Revisit date.

### 4. Options View
Compare options like neighborhoods, visas, housing approaches, schools, and insurance.

Fields:
- Option name.
- Category.
- Pros.
- Cons.
- Estimated cost.
- Risk.
- Confidence.
- Notes.

### 5. Ideas / Conversation View
A place to capture ideas and questions for later discussion.

Fields:
- Idea or question.
- Topic.
- Priority.
- Notes.
- Discussed? yes/no.
- Outcome.

### 6. Export / Snapshot View
Generate snapshots of the current plan.

Exports:
- Full planning snapshot PDF.
- Budget PDF.
- Decisions PDF.
- Roadmap PDF.
- Open questions PDF.
- JSON data export.
